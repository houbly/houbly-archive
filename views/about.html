Quick Bio

One of my favourite things to do as a kid was to draw, and I was always pretty good at it. At about 16 years old I met Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator and was mesmerised, so I spent a lot of time learning to use them. Eventually I realised that I was most interested in designing and making.

In time I had a good handle on quite a few graphic design tools and eventually went on to study Interactive Media at Nottingham Trent University where was exposed to a bunch of different design practices [digital design, animation, film, video]. I took a liking to web design and animation in particular and began learning code. Not long after graduating I began working as a front end web developer and in short, that's how I've ended up working in the 'web/digital realm' since 2013.
